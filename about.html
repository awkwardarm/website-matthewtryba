About

From Global Hits to Your Next Record

Matthew Tryba offers independent artists the one thing they need to compete: a major label sound. His foundation was built engineering for music's elite on records like the RIAA Diamond-certified "Counting Stars" (OneRepublic), the Grammy-winning "1989" (Taylor Swift), and multi-platinum albums for Adele, Ariana Grande, and Maroon 5. This masterclass in what separates a good song from a global hit is now the bedrock of his production work.

Specializing in Pop, Adult Contemporary, and Dark-Pop/Soul, Matthew partners with artists to transform their raw ideas into polished, commercially competitive records ready for radio and sync placement. His sound is built on a backbone of authentic vocals and proven song structure, elevated by textured drums and unique sound design.

Now based in Denver, CO, Matthew works with artists around the world, bringing his unique blend of technical excellence and creative partnership to every project.
